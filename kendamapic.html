Kendama Forest Picture

I created this design in my sophomore year using Adobe Photoshop. It is for a company I started in my freshman year of high school. Upon seeing how popular kendamas were becoming, I created this company for reselling kendamas. I used the photo above to advertise the products and my company. Through this project, I learned how to edit images in photoshop, make the colors more vibrant, and make photos look more high quality.
