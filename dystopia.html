Dystopia

This design was for an English project in my Sophmore year. I designed this using Adobe Photoshop. For the project, I had to demonstrate what a dystopia or a utopia would like to me. In my perspective, a dystopian society would be worn down and in ruins. I actually used and put together five different photos to make this one dystopian photo. Through this project, I learned how to mix different photos together to make it look like one.
