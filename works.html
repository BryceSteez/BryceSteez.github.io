behance competition

This “Trees and Smoke” design was for a competition that I got invited to participate in during my junior year. For the competition, I had to make a design that shows the connection between graphic and web designers. For this design, I was required include the circle and use a specific size. I utilized the red, green, and blue colors, which represents the RGB color model. I demonstrated how those colors come together in web and graphic design to make new colors.

psa underage drinking

The “Chalkboard Underage Drinking” design was a PSA to discourage underage drinking. I created this using Adobe Photoshop. This was a contest in my Web Design class in my sophomore  year of high school where we had to have a graphic and a video for the PSA. We were able to get in the top 50 of all the submissions. I learned about all the consequences that came with underage drinking. I also learned some new skills in Photoshop like using smart objects and such.

PEACE

The “Peace Design” was done simply for myself, I wanted a desktop picture for my personal laptop that was custom and something that I created. This was created in Adobe Photoshop. I just chose the word “Peace” because I was in a peaceful mood at that time. I added a lot of nature in this design to make it appear more calming. With the project, I learned that sizing matters when you are designing anything, especially since I was creating it specifically for my desktop.
