Logo Re - Design For Nazsteez

The design is a logo for a company called Nasteez. This is a clothing company I started in about 6th grade. I recreated the logo a few years after creating my logo because I wasn’t satisfied with the original. The old logo was actually created by someone else. I felt that because it was my company, I needed to use my own logo. I used Photoshop to create this design. In the design I accentuated the “A” is in the middle because, after contemplating others’ feeback, I realized that it looks much better than emphasizing an “X”, like in the original design. This project helped me learn through feedback and criticism.
