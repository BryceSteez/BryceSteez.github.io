Activities

The “activities” ad was for a project for graphic design we had to make ads that had to be animated using adobe flash. But I decided to go for something that would be more of a challenge but look better. I used a new program adobe came out my sophomore year named adobe fuse to make the 3d character. Then I brought that character into photoshop where I made the character move and then make the background go with the animation. This brought me out of my comfort zone using new programs by experimentation.
